About Me

Hi! My name is Thomas Fisher.

I have a Master's Degree in Information Systems Management and experience in technical project/product management, developing web applications, and turning side projects into businesses (with varying levels of success).

I'm passionate about using technology to solve problems, whether that be improving business processes or just building something to make my life easier. I enjoy coding when I need to but understanding a problem well enough to design a solution is what I love to do.
