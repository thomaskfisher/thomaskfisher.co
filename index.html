About Me

Hi! My name is Thomas Fisher and I'm a graduate student at Brigham Young University studying Information Systems. I'm currently living in Silicon Valley interning at Tesla and loving every minute of it.

Big dreams have always come naturally to me, and I firmly believe that you can't get anywhere in life without hard work. Whether it was learning to play an instrument twice my size in elementary school, fixing a fifty-year-old truck with absolutely no experience, or teaching myself HTML in between shifts and class, I work hard to accomplish my goals. My outlook on life is this, anything is possible if you're passionate, dedicate the necessary time, and work until the only possible outcome is success.

In my free time, I play the upright Bass, most recently in the BYU University String Orchestra and the BYU Jazz Lab Ensemble. I have played soccer since I was young, and since moving to BYU I've been playing intramural soccer on and off. I also love learning about and working on old cars. My first car was a 1964 Ford F-250 that I bought from my grandfather (the original owner), and my current baby is a 1973 Jeep CJ-5 I hope to eventually turn into a Moab-worthy rock crawler.
